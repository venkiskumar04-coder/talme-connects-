Talme Plans

Companionship support with clear activities and simple pricing.

Talme Companions connects caring young fellows with elderly grandparents who need companionship, conversation, and support in daily activities.

Every family is different, so we can personalize visits around routine, comfort, hobbies, technology help, outings, and everyday emotional support.

Quick Overview

Support that feels warm, useful, and personal

Families can choose recurring companionship, flexible hourly support, or single daily visits based on what suits their senior best.
