Talme Plans

Companionship support with clear activities and simple pricing.

Talme Companions connects caring young fellows with elderly grandparents who need companionship, conversation, and support in daily activities.

Every family is different, so we can personalize visits around routine, comfort, hobbies, technology help, outings, and everyday emotional support.

Quick Overview

Support that feels warm, useful, and personal

Families can choose recurring companionship, flexible hourly support, or single daily visits based on what suits their senior best.

What We Can Help With

Meaningful time together, shaped around the senior's day.

At-home companionship

  • Simply talking and spending time together
  • Playing carrom and chess
  • Reading newspapers and sharing conversation
  • Festival activities and hobbies

Support outside the home

  • Vegetable and medicine shopping
  • Hospital visits and checkups
  • Accompanied walks and simple outings
  • Errands with patient, caring company

Everyday assistance

  • Helping with mobile phones and basic tech
  • Gentle encouragement for daily routines
  • Consistent social interaction and emotional ease
  • Personalized companionship based on comfort
