We Are Talme Connects

Companionship as a profession, care as a value.

As a member of our team, you will receive ongoing training and thoughtful support to help you grow both professionally and personally. We work with every team member to identify strengths, deepen emotional intelligence, and build the skills required to thrive in a role centered on trust and companionship.

You will also have the opportunity to make a real difference in the lives of seniors and experience the deep satisfaction that comes from knowing your work has brought comfort, presence, and joy into someone's day.

Together, we can bring companionship, confidence, and warmth into the lives of senior citizens while building a culture of care that feels premium, purposeful, and deeply human.

What you can expect

  • Ongoing training and mentorship
  • A role centered on empathy and meaningful impact
  • Professional growth with structured support
  • The opportunity to build lasting human relationships
