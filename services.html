Talme Services

Warm, thoughtful support that feels like family.

Welcome to our intergenerational family, where Talme Companions is here to provide warmth, companionship, and the kind of attention that feels personal and reassuring. Along with meaningful bonds, here are some of the ways our seniors enjoy spending time with their young companions.

Please note: We are not limited to these services. Every Talme Companions plan is tailored thoughtfully to create a personalized experience for each senior and family.

Personalized Care

Choose support that fits your senior's day

From companionship and outings to technology help and errands, every Talme plan is shaped around comfort, routine, personality, and emotional ease.

Talme Companions

Discover the joy of quality time

Be it simple chitchat, reminiscing about old stories, sharing worries, enjoying biscuits and tea, playing indoor games, or simply watching television together, our companions are always ready to listen with patience and warmth.

Technology Support

Bridge the generation gap with tech-savvy youth

Your Talme companion will sit with you for as long as needed to explain smartphones, gadgets, social media basics, video calls, online orders, digital payments, and day-to-day digital habits in a calm and easy way.

Outdoor Companionship

Find joy in walking together

We love a good walk and would be glad to keep you company, whether that means strolling in the park, escorting you to a friend's home, joining you for a temple visit, or simply becoming a familiar and friendly presence on your favorite route.

Daily Support

Companionship for everyday tasks

Picking up groceries, buying medicines, shopping at the mall, or going to a doctor's clinic can all feel lighter with someone beside you. Talme companions make caring, dependable company for those everyday outings.

Experiences

Discover the thrill of something new

Try that lovely restaurant, watch a new film, explore a hobby, revisit an old favorite, or spend an afternoon somewhere refreshing. Our companions can help turn ordinary time into memorable outings.

Gatherings

Join us for exclusive events and community evenings

What's better than one companion? A room full of warm-hearted companions and seniors coming together for thoughtfully hosted evenings of food, conversation, and shared fun.
